History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of developers in Lagos. Over the years, it has grown into an international event that brings together innovators, students, and professionals to explore the future of technology.

Mission

Our mission is to empower young minds and bridge the digital divide by making technology accessible, engaging, and inclusive for all—especially those under 15 who are eager to build the future.
